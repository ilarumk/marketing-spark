# What Are Metrics?

<span className="badge badge-beginner">Beginner</span>

Creating great content is only half the job. You also need to know if it's working. That's where metrics come in - the numbers that tell the story of your marketing success.

---

## Why Numbers Matter

Imagine you post something on Instagram. You think it's great. But how do you know if it actually worked?

Without metrics, you're guessing. With metrics, you know:
- Did people see it?
- Did they care?
- Did they take action?

<div className="tip-box">
  <strong>The Point:</strong> Metrics help you make better decisions. Instead of guessing what works, you can see the evidence.
</div>

---

## What Are Metrics?

**Metrics** are measurements that show how your marketing is performing.

Think of them like a report card for your content:
- How many people saw it (reach)
- How many people interacted (engagement)
- How many took action (conversions)

Every social media platform tracks these numbers for you.

---

## Basic Metric Categories

### 1. Awareness Metrics
*How many people saw your content?*

- **Reach** - Number of unique people who saw your post
- **Impressions** - Total times your post was displayed
- **Views** - For video content, how many times it was watched

### 2. Engagement Metrics
*Did people interact with your content?*

- **Likes** - Simple appreciation
- **Comments** - People took time to respond
- **Shares/Reposts** - They wanted others to see it
- **Saves** - They found it valuable enough to save

### 3. Growth Metrics
*Is your audience growing?*

- **Followers gained** - New people following you
- **Followers lost** - People who unfollowed
- **Net growth** - Gained minus lost

### 4. Action Metrics
*Did people do what you wanted?*

- **Link clicks** - They clicked through
- **Website visits** - They went to your site
- **Conversions** - They took a specific action

---

## Understanding What Numbers Mean

Numbers alone don't tell the whole story. Context matters.

### Example: 100 Likes

Is 100 likes good or bad?

**For an account with 500 followers:** That's great! 20% of your audience engaged.

**For an account with 50,000 followers:** That's not great. Only 0.2% engaged.

<div className="fun-fact">
  <strong>This Is Why Percentages Matter:</strong> Engagement rate (likes + comments + shares ÷ followers × 100) tells you more than raw numbers.
</div>

## Metrics on Different Platforms

Each platform has its own analytics dashboard:

### Instagram Insights
- Available on business/creator accounts (free to switch)
- Shows reach, engagement, follower demographics
- Access: Profile → Professional Dashboard → Insights

### TikTok Analytics
- Available on business accounts
- Shows views, likes, comments, shares
- Access: Profile → Menu → Creator Tools → Analytics

### YouTube Studio
- Detailed analytics for channels
- Shows views, watch time, subscribers
- Access: YouTube Studio dashboard

### Facebook Insights
- Page-level analytics
- Shows reach, engagement, followers
- Access: Page → Insights

## What to Track (and What to Ignore)

### Track These (They Matter)
| Metric | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------------|
| Engagement rate | Shows if people actually care |
| Reach | Are you getting in front of people? |
| Saves | Sign of valuable content |
| Comments | Real interaction |
| Click-throughs | Did they take action? |

### Don't Obsess Over These
| Metric | Why It's Less Important |
|--------|------------------------|
| Total followers | Quality > quantity |
| Likes alone | Easy to give, doesn't mean much |
| Impressions | Seeing ≠ caring |

<div className="important-box">
  <strong>Vanity Metrics Warning:</strong> Follower count and likes feel good but don't always mean success. 1,000 engaged followers beat 10,000 unengaged ones.
</div>

---

## When to Check Metrics

Don't check every hour! Set a schedule:

| Frequency | What to Check |
|-----------|--------------|
| Daily | Quick glance at recent post performance |
| Weekly | Overall engagement trends, follower growth |
| Monthly | Deep dive, compare to previous month |

---

## Key Terms to Know

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Reach** | Unique people who saw content |
| **Impressions** | Total times content was displayed |
| **Engagement** | Total interactions (likes, comments, shares, saves) |
| **Engagement Rate** | Engagement ÷ Reach × 100 |
| **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** | Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100 |
| **Conversion** | When someone takes a desired action |
| **Analytics** | The data/numbers about your performance |
