## Pricing Philosophy

Paws & Claws is NOT the cheapest option. Their philosophy:

> "We compete on value, not price. Our customers come to us for quality products, expert advice, and a relationship - not because we're the cheapest."

### Price Position
- 10-20% higher than big chains
- Comparable to other specialty pet stores
- Focus on premium and natural products

### How They Justify Prices
- Personal service and expertise
- Curated, quality products
- Community involvement
- Knowing customers by name
- Returns/exchanges without hassle
