# About Paws & Claws Pet Shop

This is the fictional business you'll be helping throughout the course. Get to know them well - understanding the business is the first step to creating great marketing!

---

## Business Overview

**Business Name:** Paws & Claws Pet Shop
**Type:** Local pet supply store with grooming services
**Location:** 123 Main Street, Riverside (fictional town)
**Founded:** 2015
**Employees:** 5 (2 full-time, 3 part-time)

---

## The Story

Paws & Claws was started by Maria and David Chen in 2015. After struggling to find quality pet supplies and personalized advice at big chain stores, they decided to open their own shop.

What started as a small store has grown into a beloved community hub for pet owners. They're known for their knowledgeable staff, quality products, and genuine love for animals.

<div className="tip-box">
  <strong>Their Motto:</strong> "Where every pet is family."
</div>

---

## Mission & Values

### Mission Statement
"To provide pet owners with quality products, expert advice, and a welcoming community where every pet is treated like family."

### Core Values
1. **Quality First** - Only stock products they'd use for their own pets
2. **Community** - Support local shelters and pet adoption
3. **Education** - Help pet owners make informed decisions
4. **Personal Touch** - Know customers and their pets by name
5. **Compassion** - Treat every animal with love and respect

---

## What Makes Them Different

Unlike big chain pet stores, Paws & Claws offers:

| Big Chain Stores | Paws & Claws |
|------------------|--------------|
| Generic advice | Personalized recommendations |
| Focus on sales | Focus on what's best for the pet |
| Impersonal experience | Know your pet's name |
| Limited product knowledge | Deep expertise |
| Mass-market products | Curated quality selection |

## Business Goals

### Short-Term (Next 6 Months)
- Increase social media following by 50%
- Drive more foot traffic to the store
- Promote grooming services (currently underutilized)
- Host successful monthly community events

### Long-Term (1-2 Years)
- Become the go-to pet shop in Riverside
- Build strong online presence
- Expand grooming services
- Consider opening second location

---

## Current Challenges

### Marketing Challenges
- Limited social media presence (only 1,200 followers)
- Competing with big chains on price
- Getting the word out about events
- Converting online engagement to store visits

### Business Challenges
- Limited marketing budget
- Small team (can't dedicate full-time to marketing)
- Competing with online pet retailers (Chewy, Amazon)
- Building awareness in a growing town

---

## Target Market

### Primary Audience
- Pet owners within 15-mile radius
- Age 25-55
- Care about quality over cheapest price
- Value personal service and expertise

### Secondary Audiences
- Families looking to adopt their first pet
- Pet owners seeking grooming services
- People interested in natural/organic pet products
- Local rescue organizations
