## Welcome, Future Marketers!

Have you ever wondered how your favorite brands show up on your social media feed? Or why some posts get millions of likes while others don't? **Digital marketing** is the answer - and you're about to learn how it all works.

This course will teach you the same skills that real marketing professionals use every day. By the end, you'll have the knowledge to help a local business, volunteer for a nonprofit, or even start promoting your own creative projects.

## Who Is This For?

This course is perfect for you if:

- You're in **middle school** (grades 6-8) or around that age
- You're curious about how social media and advertising work
- You want to learn skills that could help you **volunteer** for a local business or nonprofit
- You're interested in marketing, business, or creative careers
- You just want to understand the digital world better

## About This Course

Marketing Spark was created to help young people understand the digital marketing world. All the skills you learn here are real skills used by marketing professionals every day.

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:
- Explain how social media marketing works
- Create content that connects with an audience
- Read and understand basic analytics reports
- Plan a simple marketing campaign
- Have a portfolio piece to show potential volunteer opportunities
