# Activity: Your First Campaign

<span className="badge badge-beginner">Beginner</span>

Time to put everything together! In this activity, you'll create a simple social media campaign for Paws & Claws Pet Shop.

---

## Your Mission

Paws & Claws is hosting an **Adoption Day Event** next month. They're partnering with a local animal shelter to find homes for rescue pets.

Your job: Create a social media campaign to promote this event and get people to attend.

---

## Campaign Brief

**Event:** Paws & Claws Adoption Day
**Date:** Saturday, 10 AM - 4 PM
**Location:** Paws & Claws Pet Shop (123 Main Street)
**Goal:** Get people to attend and adopt pets
**Partner:** Happy Tails Animal Shelter

**What's happening:**
- 15-20 dogs and cats available for adoption
- Reduced adoption fees ($50 instead of $150)
- Free starter kit (food, toy, leash) with every adoption
- Meet-and-greet area for families
- Pet care Q&A with a veterinarian

## Bonus Challenges

Want to go further? Try these:

### Challenge 1: Design the Graphics
Sketch out what your announcement post would look like. What colors? What text? What images?

### Challenge 2: Write All the Captions
Write complete captions for all 5 content pieces, including hashtags.

### Challenge 3: Plan the Hashtags
Research and list 10-15 hashtags you'd use for this campaign.

Suggestions to start:
- #AdoptDontShop
- #PetAdoption
- #RescuePets
- #[YourCityName]Pets

### Challenge 4: Create a Video Script
Write a 30-second script for the "Meet the Pets" video, including:
- What to say
- What to show
- When to add text
