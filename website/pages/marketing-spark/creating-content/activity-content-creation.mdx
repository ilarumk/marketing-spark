## Review Checklist

Before considering your content "done," check each piece:

<div className="checklist">

**For every post:**
- Does it provide value (educate, entertain, or inspire)?
- Is the first line/second attention-grabbing?
- Would you stop scrolling to see this?
- Does it match the Paws & Claws brand voice?
- Is there a clear call-to-action?

**For written content:**
- Is it easy to read (short paragraphs, line breaks)?
- Does it sound like a real person, not a robot?
- Are relevant hashtags included?

**For visual content:**
- Is it high quality and professional-looking?
- Is text readable?
- Does it follow basic design principles?

</div>
