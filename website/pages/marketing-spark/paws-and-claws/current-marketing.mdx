# Current Marketing

Here's what Paws & Claws is currently doing for marketing. Understanding their baseline helps you make strategic recommendations!

---

## Social Media Presence

### Instagram (@pawsandclawsriverside)

**Current Status:**
- 1,200 followers
- Posting 2-3 times per week
- Mix of photos and occasional Reels
- Stories 3-4 times per week

**What's Working:**
- Cute pet photos get good engagement
- Adoption event posts perform well
- Grooming before/after photos are popular

**What's Not Working:**
- Product-only posts get low engagement
- Inconsistent posting schedule
- Hashtag strategy is random
- No content calendar

**Example Recent Posts:**
| Post | Type | Engagement |
|------|------|------------|
| Grooming transformation | Photo | 95 likes, 8 comments |
| "New dog food in stock" | Photo | 22 likes, 0 comments |
| Adoption event announcement | Carousel | 78 likes, 15 comments |
| Happy customer & pet | Photo | 110 likes, 12 comments |
| Store hours reminder | Graphic | 15 likes, 0 comments |

## What the Owner Wants

Maria's marketing wishlist:

1. **More foot traffic** - Especially on weekdays
2. **Grooming bookings** - Currently at 60% capacity
3. **Event attendance** - Want 50+ people at adoption days
4. **Social growth** - "I see other pet shops with 10k+ followers"
5. **Local awareness** - "People drive past us to go to PetMart"
