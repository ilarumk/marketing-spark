## Sample Analysis (Check Your Work)

<div className="activity-box">
  <strong>Reference Answers:</strong>

  **Basic Calculations:**
  - Reach increase: 86% ((15,800 - 8,500) ÷ 8,500 × 100)
  - Follower increase: 21% ((1,333 - 1,100) ÷ 1,100 × 100)
  - Reel engagement rate: 10% (185 ÷ 1,850 × 100)
  - Carousel engagement rate: 9% (88 ÷ 980 × 100)
  - Photo engagement rate: 6.9% (36 ÷ 520 × 100)
  - Best performer: Reels (10%)
  - Engagement rate: Stable (improved 1 point then held)

  **Key Insights:**
  - Reels crush other formats (3.5x the reach of photos)
  - Educational/helpful content performs best
  - Generic/promotional content performs worst
  - December boost likely from holiday content + more posting
  - Audience is primarily women 25-44 in local area

  **Smart Recommendations:**
  - Create 3+ Reels per week
  - Focus on tips, education, and announcements
  - Stop posting generic "Happy Monday" content
  - Post weekday evenings and weekend mornings
  - Q1 Goal: Reach 20,000/month, 1,500 followers
</div>
