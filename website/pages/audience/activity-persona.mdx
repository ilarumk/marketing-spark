## Your Assignment

Create **2 detailed personas** for Paws & Claws. These should represent two different types of customers who would shop there.

---

## Persona 1: Your Choice

Think about what type of customer Paws & Claws might serve. Here are some ideas:

- First-time pet owner
- Senior with a companion pet
- Family with young kids and pets
- Busy professional with a dog
- Cat person who spoils their fur baby
- Someone interested in adopting

Choose ONE type or create your own!
