## Your Assignment

Create a **$50 mock ad campaign** for Paws & Claws Pet Shop to promote their Weekend Adoption Event.

---

## Campaign Brief

**Event:** Weekend Adoption Event
**Date:** Saturday & Sunday, 10am - 4pm
**Location:** Paws & Claws Pet Shop
**Partner:** Happy Tails Animal Shelter

**What's happening:**
- 20+ dogs and cats available for adoption
- Reduced adoption fees ($75 instead of $150)
- Free starter kit with every adoption
- Pet care Q&A with a veterinarian
- Face painting for kids

**Goal:** Get at least 50 people to attend

**Budget:** $50

## Sample Campaign (Check Your Work)

Here's one possible approach:

<div className="lesson-card">

### Sample Campaign: Adoption Event

**Goal:** Get 50+ people to attend the adoption event

**Platform:** Instagram (primary), with Story + Feed ads

**Targeting:**
- Age: 22-55
- Location: Within 15 miles of store
- Interests: Pet adoption, dogs, cats, animal rescue, pet lovers
- Behaviors: Engaged with pet content

**Budget Split:**
- $30 on Feed/Reel ad (awareness)
- $20 on Stories (last 2 days, reminder)

**Ad Creative:**
- Video showing adorable adoptable pets with text overlay
- Hook: "These pets are looking for their forever homes"
- CTA: Get Directions

**Primary Text:**
"This weekend only! 20+ adorable pets are waiting to meet you at our Adoption Day event.

Reduced fees ($75), free starter kit, and our team will help you find your perfect match.

Saturday & Sunday, 10am-4pm at Paws & Claws Pet Shop.

Will you be the one to change their life?"

**Success Metrics:**
- Primary: Event attendance (goal: 50+)
- Secondary: Link clicks, reach, engagement
- Success = 50+ attendees, at least 10 adoptions

</div>
