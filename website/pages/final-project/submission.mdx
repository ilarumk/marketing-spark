## Save Your Work

Your marketing plan is a portfolio piece. Here's how to preserve it:

### Option 1: Digital Document
- Save as PDF for easy sharing
- Keep the original editable version too
- Store in cloud storage (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.)

### Option 2: Portfolio Website
- Create a simple portfolio page
- Include screenshots of key sections
- Write a brief case study about your approach

### Option 3: Physical Copy
- Print a clean copy
- Keep in a folder with other work samples
- Great for in-person opportunities

## Opportunities for Young Marketers

### Volunteer Ideas
- Local small businesses
- Nonprofits and charities
- School events and clubs
- Community organizations
- Family friends' businesses

### How to Approach Them

1. **Be professional** - Treat it like a real opportunity
2. **Lead with value** - Show how you can help them
3. **Set clear expectations** - What you'll do, how often
4. **Ask for feedback** - Learn from real results
5. **Document your work** - Build your portfolio

### What You Can Offer
- Social media content creation
- Content calendar planning
- Basic analytics reporting
- Graphic design (Canva)
- Content ideas and strategy

## A Note on Age & Platform Rules

<div className="important-box">
  <strong>Important Reminder:</strong>

  - Most platforms require users to be 13+
  - Business accounts may have different requirements
  - Ad accounts typically require 18+ and payment method
  - Always get parent/guardian permission
  - Don't access accounts you shouldn't have access to

  Until you're 18, focus on:
  - Learning the strategy and concepts
  - Creating sample content
  - Building your knowledge base
  - Helping with supervision
